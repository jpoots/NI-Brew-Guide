Welcome!

Here at NI Brew Guide we care about one thing above all, coffee done right. Speciality coffee made by trained staff who can get a delcious cup from the raw materials.

As my countrymen will know, we have this in abundance for such a small country. This website seeks to serve this scene and its frequenters along with supporting lesser known coffee shops from around the country which get missed due to not being in Belfast or on the North Coast.

While we admit shops based soley on coffee, we encourage our user to leave reviews about the overall experience to give others an idea. You can rest assured any shop on this site reaches the standards of the admin.

Please, enjoy using the website at your leisure.

- Jordan
